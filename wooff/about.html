ABOUT US

We are a group of friends who love dogs and are trying to create a local community for people with the same love for dogs! We have created this page for locals to be able to access the community easily and look for events such as dog meets, dog adoptions, charity help etc. Our goal is to help dogs in any way we can. We hope you can be a part of this journey!
